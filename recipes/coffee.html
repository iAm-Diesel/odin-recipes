Coffee recipe for your kitchen

Descripton

Coffee is a lovely way to start the day. Everyone should drink coffee, but not everyone knows how to make it. Here is a simple recipe for coffee that you can easily make at home!

Ingredients Needed

  • Water
  • Ground Coffee
  • Pot to boil water OR a coffee machine
  • Paper coffee filters

Steps

  1. Start by pouring water into the pot and heating it on the stove. Remove just before it starts to boil.
  2. While the water is heating up, take your coffee filter and put it into your coffee machine or pourover filter holder.
  3. Fill the filter with a few scoops of ground coffee
  4. Once water is about to boil, take it off the stove and pour it over the coffee grounds
  5. Let the coffee cool, and enjoy!
