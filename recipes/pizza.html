Homemade pizza that is easy and fun for everyone

Description

Pizza is a great treat for the entire family to enjoy for lunch or dinner. It's also a lot easer to make than you might think. Here's a wonderful recipe to get you and your family making homemade pizza today!

Ingredients Needed

  • Dough
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Toppings of choice: Pepperoni, ham, pinneapple, ect.

Steps

  1. First, kneed the dough into a pizza shape and size of your liking
  2. Before you move on, preheat your oven to 425 degrees
  3. Next, take the tomatoe sauce and spread it out over the dough
  4. Third, take the shredded cheese and sprinkle it over the tomatoe sauce, as much as you want
  5. Then, take whatever topping you have chosen and put those on top of the shredded cheese
  6. Now, take the precooked pizza and place it on a pizza pan
  7. Place that pizza pan with the pizza on it into an oven
  8. Let the pizza cook for 30-40 minutes
  9. Carefully take out of oven, cut into slices and enjoy!
